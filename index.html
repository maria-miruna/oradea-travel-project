Why Oradea?

Oradea, where heritage meets modernity

Oradea is a city full of magic, tranquility, elegance and beauty. Oradea is one of the important economic, social and cultural centers in NW Romania, preserving these characteristics throughout history.

Along with the representative buildings in baroque, classical, secession or eclectic style, the city also lives through cultural and entertainment events such as "Festum Varadinum", the Fair of Folk Craftsmen, the Citadel Celebrations, the "Autumn Festival" and "Short Theater Week". Also, nightlife in Oradea is active. The historic city center abounds in terraces, cafes, restaurants and clubs. Most of them are located in monument buildings and the atmosphere given by them make the experience unique.
